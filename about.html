Hey there! I'm Craia Bogdan, a 20-year-old student at the Faculty of Automatic Control and Computers in Bucharest. Here, I'm diving deep into my love for technology and programming.

I was born in the beautiful city of Călărași, where I grew up in a lively and colorful community. From a young age, I was drawn to the fascinating world of photography. With each captured frame, I discovered that I could immortalize emotions and stories in a unique and personal way.

My passion for photography merged with an interest in computers and technology. I began to explore how these two fields can interact and complement each other. Thus, I developed skills in digital photo editing and image processing.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring different places, discovering landscapes, and capturing unique moments in photographs. This hobby motivates me to see the world from new perspectives and share these experiences with others.

Besides that, I am dedicated to my studies in the field of automation, and I am fascinated by the potential of emerging technologies. I can't wait to see how I can contribute and innovate in this ever-evolving field. If you'd like to discuss, feel free to contact me via email.
